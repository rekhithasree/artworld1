Media arts

New media art includes artworks designed and produced by means of new media technologies, comprising virtual art, computer graphics, computer animation, digital art, interactive art, sound art, Internet art, video games, robotics, 3D printing, and cyborg art

  • 1
    Installation art

    Installation art is an artistic genre of three-dimensional works that are often site-specific and designed to transform the perception of a space.

  • 2
    Films

    Film, also called motion picture or movie, series of still photographs on film, projected in rapid succession onto a screen by means of light.

  • 2
    Digital art

    Digital art can be computer generated, scanned or drawn using a tablet and a mouse.
